The classic drug dealing simulation game

Based on the original by John E. Dell (1984)

How to Play

You have 30 days to make as much money as possible buying and selling goods across New York City.

Basics

  • Travel between 6 locations - prices vary by location and day
  • Buy low, sell high - watch for price spikes and crashes
  • You start with $5,500 cash and a $5,500 debt to the Loan Shark
  • Your trench coat holds 100 units - upgrade when you can

The Loan Shark

  • Your debt grows 10% daily - pay it off fast!
  • You can borrow more if you need startup capital
  • Visit the Loan Shark from any location

The Bank

  • Deposit cash to keep it safe from muggers
  • Withdraw when you need buying power

Random Events

  • Price spikes and crashes create huge profit opportunities
  • Watch out for police and muggers!
  • You might find free goods or a chance to upgrade your coat

Tips

  • Pay off your debt ASAP - compound interest is brutal
  • Expensive items have wider price swings = more profit potential
  • Always keep some cash for unexpected deals
